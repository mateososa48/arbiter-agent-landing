How it works

Set it once.
Let it run.

Three steps from setup to fully automated referee scheduling. No recurring manual work required.

01
🎛
Tell It What You Want
Set your preferences once: minimum pay, how far you'll travel, which positions you do, and what age groups you prefer. Set a high bar for auto-accept and a lower bar for "email me, I'll decide." That's it — you're done with setup.

02
🤖
It Checks Every Hour, Automatically
Every hour, the agent logs into ArbiterSports and pulls every available game. Each one gets scored 0–100 against your criteria. If a game overlaps with something already on your calendar, it's automatically skipped — no double-bookings, ever.

Features

Built for referees
who are busy.

Every feature exists to eliminate manual work and maximize the games you get.

🎯
AI Scores Every Game
Every available game gets a 0–100 score based on your preferences — pay, distance, position, age group, and league. You can see exactly why each game scored the way it did.
⚡
Auto-Assigns the Best Ones
Games above your threshold are claimed to your ArbiterSports account instantly — the same as clicking "Go" yourself, but done while you sleep. First come, first served. You come first.
📅
Skips Conflicting Games
Syncs with your existing calendar. If a game overlaps with something you already have — including travel buffer time — it's automatically excluded. No accidental double-bookings.
📧
Email Digest for Close Calls
Games just below your auto-assign bar get emailed to you in a clean summary: score, reasoning, pay, distance, and a direct link. Review in 30 seconds and decide.
🎚
Two Thresholds, Your Control
Set a high bar to auto-claim and a lower bar to get notified. "Great" games are handled automatically. "Good" games land in your inbox. Everything else is silently ignored.
🔒
No Password Required
Setup takes one terminal command to capture your browser session. No passwords stored anywhere. When your session expires (~2 weeks), you get an alert email to renew in seconds.
